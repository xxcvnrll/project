    CHICKEN SOUP WITH HOMEMADE NOODLES

    INGREDIENTS:

    • onion ‚Äì 2 pcs
    • chicken ‚Äì 800g
    • celera ‚Äì 2 pcs
    • water ‚Äì 1.5 liter of water
    • green onion, parsley to taste*

    PREPARATION:

    1. Place the chicken, one onion, one carrot, and celery in a saucepan. Cover with water.
    2. Bring to a boil, simmer chicken broth for 1.5 hours with lid on.
    3. To prepare the noodles, beat 6 egg yolks and 1 egg with a whisk, adding salt and water.
    4. Place the dough on a board and knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth.
    5. Disassemble and lay out the noodles on a board and leave to dry for 20-30 minutes.
    6. Cut the second onion into cubes and the remaining carrots into strips.
    7. Add chopped carrots and onions to the broth. Add bay leaf, peppercorns and salt. Cook over low heat for 10 minutes.

    FUNCHOZA WITH CHICKEN, ZUCCHINI AND SWEET PEPPER

    INGREDIENTS:

    • Funchoza ‚Äì 100g
    • chicken fillet ‚Äì 250g
    • zucchini - 100g
    • red bell pepper‚Äì 80g
    • yellow bell pepper ‚Äì 100g
    • soy sause ‚Äì 40 ml
    • sunflower oil ‚Äì 30 ml

    PREPARATION:

    1. Rinse the chicken fillet and dry it with a paper towel. Cut the fillet into thin strips.
    2. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a deep frying pan or wok and add the chopped chicken meat. Pour in 20 ml of soy sauce.
    3. While the chicken is cooking, cut the zucchini into thin strips.

    5. Pour some vegetable oil into the frying pan, add chopped bell peppers, hot peppers and funchoza. Stir.
    6. Funchoza with chicken, zucchini and sweet pepper is ready to serve.

    NOODLES WITH SHRIMP AND VEGETABLE

    INGREDIENTS:

    • king shrimp ‚Äì 12 pcs
    • carrots ‚Äì 50g
    • ginger - 30g
    • ramen noodles‚Äì 200g
    • lemon juice ‚Äì 2 tbsp
    • green onion, cilantro springs

    PREPARATION:

    1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok, add carrots and fry. After 1 minute, add zucchini, fry and remove all ingredients.
    2. Add the shrimp with 1 tbsp oil to the remaining vegetable fat, fry for 3 minutes, then remove from the pan.
    3. In the remaining hot oil in the wok, fry the ginger and garlic. Add the vegetables and shrimp back to the pan and fry briefly.
    4. Add lemon juice, Kikkoman soy sauce and Kikkoman sesame oil, then the cooked noodles and heat the whole dish gently.
    5. Sprinkle with lemon zest, green onions and coriander leaves and serve!
