PASTA IN TOMATO SAUSE

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely grated
  • 2 cans whole peeled tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

PREPARATION:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated carrot (if using), and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 1-2 minutes to deepen its flavor.
  5. Add canned tomatoes with their juices, breaking them up with a wooden spoon.
  6. Add salt to the boiling water, then add pasta and cook according to package directions until al dente.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.

LAZY COTTAGE CHEESE DUMPLINGS

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cups cottage cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp semolina or farina
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Butter for serving

PREPARATION:

  1. In a large bowl, combine cottage cheese, eggs, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Gradually add flour and semolina, stirring until a soft dough forms. The dough should be sticky but manageable.
  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a gentle boil.
  4. Using two spoons, scoop up portions of dough and drop them into the boiling water. Alternatively, you can form dumplings with floured hands.
  5. Cook the dumplings in batches without overcrowding the pot. They are ready when they float to the surface .
  6. Using a slotted spoon, remove dumplings from water and transfer to a serving plate.
  7. Serve immediately with your choice of toppings.

RATATOUILLE

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 eggplants, diced
  • 3 zucchinis, sliced
  • 2 yellow squashes, sliced
  • 2 bell peppers (red and yellow), diced
  • 4 tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced

PREPARATION:

  1. Prepare all vegetables as directed. Salt the eggplant and let it sit for 20 minutes to draw out bitterness, then rinse and pat dry.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large Dutch oven or heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat.
  3. Add onions and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add bell peppers and cook for 5 minutes until they begin to soften.
  5. Stir in herbs, salt, and pepper. Drizzle with remaining olive oil.
  6. Cover and simmer over low heat for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally, until all vegetables are tender.

VAMPIRE PASSION SMOOTHIE

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup frozen strawberries, raspberries
  • 1 small beet, peeled and chopped
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup pomegranate juice
  • 1/4 cup vanilla yogurt

PREPARATION:

  1. Prepare all ingredients. If using fresh beet, steam it for 10 minutes to soften slightly.
  2. Add pomegranate juice to the blender first, followed by yogurt.
  3. Add frozen berries, chopped beet, and banana.
  4. Blend on high until smooth and creamy. If too thick, add a splash more juice or water.
  5. Add chia seeds and pulse a few times to combine but not fully blend (to maintain texture).
  6. Taste and add sweetener if desired. For extra blood-red color, add a drop or two of red food coloring.
  7. Pour into glasses and serve immediately with spooky garnishes.
